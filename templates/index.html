About the project

This platform uses computer vision to detect drowsiness and fatigue in real time. When the system identifies signs of tiredness, it provides alerts and helps you stay energized with hydration reminders.

The goal is to improve concentration, reduce eye strain, and support healthier study sessions.

Why this system matters

It helps keep your study sessions safe and productive by detecting drowsiness, tracking fatigue, and reminding you to hydrate.
